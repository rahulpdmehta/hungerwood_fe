CUSTOM PANDA CHEF IMAGE INSTRUCTIONS
=====================================

To use your own panda chef image (like the one in your splash screen design):

1. SAVE YOUR IMAGE
   - Save your panda chef image as: panda-chef.png (or .jpg)
   - Place it in this folder: /public/

2. UPDATE SPLASHSCREEN.JSX
   - Open: src/components/common/SplashScreen.jsx
   - Find the section with comment: "/* Panda Chef Illustration */"
   - Replace the CSS-based panda with:

   <img 
     src="/panda-chef.png" 
     alt="HungerWood Panda Chef"
     className="w-full h-full object-contain p-4"
   />

3. OPTIMAL IMAGE SPECS
   - Size: 512x512px or 1024x1024px
   - Format: PNG (with transparent background) or JPEG
   - File size: Under 200KB for best performance
   - Style: Match the wooden frame aesthetic

4. EXAMPLE CODE
   Replace lines 39-103 in SplashScreen.jsx with:

   <div className="w-48 h-48 bg-gradient-to-b from-amber-400 to-amber-500 rounded-xl flex items-center justify-center relative overflow-hidden">
     {/* Wood grain pattern background */}
     <div className="absolute inset-0 opacity-20">
       <div className="w-full h-full bg-[repeating-linear-gradient(0deg,transparent,transparent_2px,rgba(0,0,0,0.1)_2px,rgba(0,0,0,0.1)_4px)]"></div>
     </div>
     
     {/* Your custom panda chef image */}
     <img 
       src="/panda-chef.png" 
       alt="HungerWood Panda Chef"
       className="w-full h-full object-contain relative z-10 p-2"
     />
   </div>

5. TEST
   - Save the file
   - Refresh your browser
   - Clear cache if needed (Ctrl+Shift+R)
